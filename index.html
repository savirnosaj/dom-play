Woman: I’ve been thinking lately.

Man: About?

Woman: A lot of things. And very few things. Does that make sense?

Man: It does to me.

She smiles.

Woman: I thought it might.

Man: I wonder if this is what we’re looking for, understanding.

Man: It’s our first time out alone. I knew her friends, she knew mine. Every now and then we’d bump into each other and exchange small talk.

Man: Hows your day? Hows work? What have you been up to? Anything new?

Man: We spoke, but never really said anything.

Man: Still, I was drawn to her.

Man: I knew she had more to say.

Man:With some people you just know.

Man: “So what’s something you’ve been thinking about?”

She shuffles through her thoughts, trying to find the right topic.

Woman: “Buildings.”
